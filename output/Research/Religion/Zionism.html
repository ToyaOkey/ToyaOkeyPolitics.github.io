Modern Zionism

Emerged in the 19th century as a response to the large antisemitism faced by the Jewish community.

Currently, advocates that a modern Jewish state would prove Jewish people with a safe haven from the discrimination suffered and ensure Jewish people have the right to nationhood and self-determination and to freely practice Judaism.

Late 1800s Theodor Herzl was coined the "father" of Zionism supporting the right of the democratic state of Israel to exist as a Jewish Homeland.

Zionism seems to be an umbrella term containing people that are moderate - progressive especially on Israel's policy.

Zionism does not exclude supporting Palestine as an independent state and right for self-determination and statehood as some Zionists support a Two State Solution

What caused modern Zionism?

Rising antisemitism tropes such as stating that jewish populatin had strong jewish ties to finance and industry led to the push for pro Zionist movements.
