Forum Analysis

Types of Forums Rules Allows Example(s)
Traditional Public Forum government grands all freedom of speech meaning no content based violation based off te The government cannot restrict any speech there are exceptions Public parks
Non Public Forum government property since not disclosed as public has limited violation The government can allow content based violation but not viewpoint based violation Schools, Library
Designated Public Forum non public area of the government that opens itself up to the public The government must follow all rules of a traditional public forum A school thats opens up an afterschool discussion to the public
Limited Public Forum Usually for more commercial stuff intermediate between non public forum and public forum. The government allows for content based restriction but not viewpoint based restriction Usually afterschool activities like sports, recreation for students

Content-based means restriction of the content of what is being said a viewpoint based violation is a restriction that stems from an opinion of the content based.

The government could restrict discussing politics in a non public forum but they cannot solely ban republic or democrat politics.

The government can put restrictions on the manner, time and place of the speech however.
