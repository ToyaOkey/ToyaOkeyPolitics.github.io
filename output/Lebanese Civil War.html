Lebanese christians and muslims were in the area of lebanon. Lebanon's muslims comprised a large minority and with the influx of thousands of palestianians in 1948 and 1967 contributed to lebanon's demographic shift. The [Cold War] also impact the conflict as the christians sided with the western world and the muslims sided with the Soviet Union. Fighting between Lebanese Christian milities and Palestinian insurgents took place first mainly from the Palestine Liberation Organization. Escalation in the conflict led to the 1982 Lebanon War
