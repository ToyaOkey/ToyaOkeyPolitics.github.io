The Cairo Agreement / Cairo Accord was an agreement between Yassar Arafet and the Lebanese army command, The agreement established principles under which the presence of activities of palestinian guerrillas in southeast lebanon would be tolered by Lebanese authorities.

Under the agreement 16 official UNRWA camps in lebanon were removed in jurisdiction in the Lebanese Army's. The agreement additionally established allowed palestinian residents in lebanon to join the palestininian revolution through armed struggle. the Palestine Liberation Organization effectively established a state within the state of lebanon.

With increased palestinian involvement within lebanon inn the 1970s the lebanese army become incapable of limiting the areas of the PLO and the Lebanese Civil War broke out between the PLO and the christians. Escalation in the conflict led to the 1982 Lebanon War
