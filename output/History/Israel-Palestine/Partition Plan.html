Overall Summary

Stemmed from the failing of the Peel Commission but also the promise of the Balfour Declaration.

Discussed the delineation of boundaries between the Arab and Jewish state with the Arab state comprising old 11,592 sq km and the Jewish state containing 15,264 while Jerusalem and Bethlehem would become an international zone while additionally calling for an economic union between the states.

The Jewish organizations collaborated with UNSCOP during the deliberations while Palestinian boycotted the movements since the Plan came off as pro Zionist since it allocated more land to the Jewish people despite there being twice as many Arabs. As a result the 1947 - 1948 Palestine Civil War broke out and the plan was not implemented. In 1948, 85% of the Palestinians living in areas that became the state of Israel became Palestinian Refugees.
