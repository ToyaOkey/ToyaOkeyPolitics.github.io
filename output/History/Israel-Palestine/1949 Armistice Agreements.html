Formal ceasefire that ended the 1948 Arab-Israeli War

The 1949 Armistice Agreements were signed between Egypt, Israel, Syria, Iraq which ended the hostilities taking place in the Arab-Israeli War and marked the "Green Line" that seperated th Arab controlled terrority from the Israel controlled territory.

  • Jordan occupied the West Bank
  • Egypt occupied Gaza Strip

The United Nations agreed to de-facto supervising and reporting agencies

The agreement was intended to only serve as interim agreements until replaced by permanent peace treaties.
