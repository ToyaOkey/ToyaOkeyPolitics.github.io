A Zionist military plan executed during the 1948 Palestine war for the conqusest of territory in Mandatory Palestine. This plan played an essential role into the formation of Palestinian Refugees and the event of Nakba.

The plan was requested by Ben-Gurion and developed by the Haganah as the plan set guidelines to take control of mandatory palestine, declare a jewish state, and defend the jewish population even the one's outside of the borders.

The tactics of the plan included laying siege to Palestinian Arab villages, bombing neighborhoods of cities, forced expulsion of inhabitants, setting fields and houses on fir, and detonating TNT in the rubble to prevent returning.

The overall plan has a lot of controversity it is often believed that it was used an an intent to genicode/expel palestinians and then take control of the whole palestine region.

Plan Dalet - Series of opertion plans which was drawn up to expand Jewish-held areas beyond the one areas already allocated. The overall objective was to take as much territory as possible in the terminate of the British Mandate
