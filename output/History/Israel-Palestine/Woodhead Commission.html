Three proposals Plan A, B, C

Plan A

  • Based on the Peel Commission
  • coast Jewish state, Jerusalem -> Jaffa
  • The remainder of Palestine would be merged into Transjordan into an Arab state

Plan B

  • Similar to plan A but reduced the size of the Jewish State by adding Galilee to the mandated area and Jaffa to the Arab state

Plan C

  • reduce the Jewish State to the coastal region between Zikhron Yaakov and Rehovot while placing northern Palestine and semi-arid part of southern Palestine under a separate mandate.
