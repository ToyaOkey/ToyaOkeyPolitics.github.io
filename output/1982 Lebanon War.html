Also called the second israeli invasion of lebanon when in 1982 Israel invaded lebanon. The invasion followed a series of attacks and counter attacks from the PLO.

The israelis sought to end the Palestinian attacks from Lebanon and destroy the Palestine Liberation Organization in the country and install a pro-israel Maronite Christian government. Israel forces attacked and overan PLO positions in south lebanon. The israeli military and the christian lebanese forces siezed control of the southern half of lebanon and siege the capital of Beirut. The PLO, were evacuated from Lebanon and the israeli government hoped to sign a treaty for peace.

A pro-israel Christian government was installed within lebanon led by President Bachir Gemayel however he was assisinated in september 1982 making the peace deal unlikely. Outrage was sparked and the Israel Defense Forces - IDF withrew from Beirut and ended its operation. The May 17 Agreement ended the state of war between Lebonan and Israel.
